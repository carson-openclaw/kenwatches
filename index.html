Our Story

Ken's Watches (名錶廊) was first set up in 2002 with our first little store in Wan Chai. We buy, we sell, and we trade watches — and we would love to share with you the fun of collecting.

For over 20 years, we've grown to 4 branches at prime locations across Hong Kong. Whether you're looking for a fashionable or collectable classic timepiece, Ken's Watches is always the place to go.

We offer the best possible price for trade-ins and a 6-month comprehensive warranty on every purchase. Our staff have profound knowledge to provide credible, professional advice.
